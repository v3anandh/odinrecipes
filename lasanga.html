    • Steps

  • Cook pasta: Boil lasagna noodles until al dente.
  • Make meat sauce: Cook ground beef, onions, and garlic, then add tomato sauce.
  • Prepare cheese: Mix ricotta cheese with parmesan cheese.
  • Assemble: Layer cooked pasta, meat sauce, and cheese mixture in a baking dish.
  • Top with cheese: Add mozzarella cheese on top.
  • Bake: Bake in the oven until cheese is melted and bubbly.
  • Serve: Serve hot and enjoy!
