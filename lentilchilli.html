Red Lentil Chilli

Description

"A hearty and flavorful stew made with red lentils, onions, garlic, and diced tomatoes, with a spicy kick from chilli powder."

Ingredians

  • lentils
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Diced tomatoes
  • Chilli powder
  • Vegetable broth
  • Salt and pepper

    • Steps

  • Saute onion and garlic
  • Add lentils and chilli powder
  • Add diced tomatoes and broth
  • Simmer until lentils are tender
  • Season with salt and pepper
  • Serve hot
